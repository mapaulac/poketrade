Welcome to PokeTrade

With the new feature allowing players to trade with one another, it has become more vital for Pokemon Go enthusiasts to be able to find others in their community and battle, trade, or collaborate.

PokeTrade is a web application that aims to provide a facilitated method for Pokemon Go players to find each other. Simply submit your contact information and the Pokemon you are looking to exchange to be part of the community!
